Welcome to the TEC Hatch Configuration Dashboard!

This tool will help YOU configure the parameters for the Hatch of the Token Engineering Commons. Your Economy, Your Choice!

Select your parameters, run them through the simulation as often as you want, and once you are happy with the results, submit your final configuration and outputs.

The community will vote on configuration submissions and use them to foster discourse in the ethical design of the TEC and other community ecosystems!

Section 1: Hatch Configuration

During the Hatch TECH tokens are minted and distributed to Backers and Builders. Backers send in wxDai and are minted TECH. If the minimum goal is reached, TECH will also be minted for the builders that earned Impact Hours. The parameters you choose below will determine the distribution.

To learn more about each parameter, mouse over the “?” for definitions or click on it for a deep dive. You can also take a look at an example Hatch Spec for a more holistic explanation.
